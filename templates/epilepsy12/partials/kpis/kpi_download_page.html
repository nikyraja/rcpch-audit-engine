KPI downloading for E12 team

KPIs are re-aggregated everytime a user enters their organisation's summary page. This ensures that the aggregations are as up to date as possible. The aggregate data itself can be calculated for different levels of location - from organisationally to nationally.

Structure of the KPI report

The KPI report is separated into 8 separate pages on an excel document. The structure of the 8 pages is as follows:

  1. Country Level
  2. HBT Level
  3. ICB Level
  4. NHS region Level
  5. Network Level
  6. National Level
  7. Reference
  8. National Comparison
